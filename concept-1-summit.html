Manifesto

Health grounds you.
Wealth frees you.
Wisdom guides you.

This podcast connects the dots. Every conversation is a search for the ideas that compound, the habits that stick, and the principles that bridge the gap between living well, building wealth and thinking clearly. No shortcuts. No hacks. Just depth.

The Three Pillars

What We
Explore

01

Healthy

Your body is your most important asset. We explore the science of longevity, performance, mental health and the daily habits that compound into a life well-lived. No fads. Just what works.

02

Wealthy

Wealth is freedom, not a scoreboard. From building businesses to managing money to understanding leverage -- we cover the strategies, mindsets and systems behind real financial independence.

03

Wise

Knowledge is knowing what to do. Wisdom is knowing when, why and whether to do it. We go deep on decision-making, philosophy, psychology and the mental models that change how you see the world.
